隱私權政策 (Privacy Policy)

  • 我們不會將您的影像或個人資訊上傳到伺服器。

  • 若您選擇透過本應用進行即時視訊連線，影像傳輸將僅用於雙方通訊，不會被記錄或存檔。

如果您對於隱私有任何疑問或希望刪除您的資料，請聯絡我們：yulinn51916@gmail.com。

English Version

Thank you for using MouseLens. We take your privacy seriously. This application only uses your camera feed to apply filters and effects. All processing happens in real time on your device and no images or data are permanently stored.

  • We do not upload your video or personal information to any server.
  • We do not share, sell, or exchange your image data with any third parties.
  • Your camera feed is processed only in your browser’s memory and will disappear once you close the application.
  • If you use this application for real-time video communication, the video is transmitted solely for peer-to-peer communication and is not recorded or stored.

If you have any questions about privacy or would like to request data deletion, please contact us at: yulinn51916@gmail.com.
